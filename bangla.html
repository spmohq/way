Bengali Content Prompts

Use the prompts below for high-quality Bengali content generation and translation.

Write in Bengali

Please write your answer in the most natural sounding Bengali (India) language that can be understood by an 11 year old but contains the international standards of marketing and language.

• Always use the formal “আপনি”, not the informal “তুমি.” The address is formal, but the tone should remain warm, conversational, and approachable.
• Write in the first person, using descriptive, natural language.
• The voice should feel like a close friend and a trusted authority, speaking one-on-one with the reader in a natural, face-to-face style.
